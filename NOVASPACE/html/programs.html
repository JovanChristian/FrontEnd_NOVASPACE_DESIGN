Our Available Programs
Space
Traveling to space is an incredible endeavor that pushes the boundaries of human exploration.
The flight duration required to reach space takes around 10 minutes for a spacecraft to reach the edge of space, where passengers can experience weightlessness and witness the curvature of the Earth.
The estimated range for the mission is a few thousand kilometers above the Earth's surface. These distances may seem vast, but they are a mere fraction of the immense scale of our universe.
Moon
Traveling to the moon is one of the humans greatest achievement.
The flight duration required to reach moon surface is around three days. This estimate includes the time it takes for the spacecraft to escape Earth's gravitational pull and enter into a trajectory towards the moon.
The estimated range for this mission is approximately 384,400 kilometers
Mars
Traveling to Mars is an extraordinary feat that represents the pinnacle of human exploration.
On average, a one-way trip to Mars takes around seven to nine months. This estimate includes the time it takes for the spacecraft to travel the vast distance between Earth and Mars.
The estimated range to Mars at their closest approach is approximately 54.6 million kilometers
